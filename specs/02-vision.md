# PRODUCT VISION

Build the centralized operational brain for warehouse automation by orchestrating robots, SMARTBox, AMR, Kardex, and warehouse workflows into a unified realtime platform with full traceability and operational visibility.

---

# PRODUCT MISSION

Deliver a reliable and scalable orchestration platform that:

* automates MRV-to-delivery workflow
* integrates heterogeneous automation systems
* provides realtime monitoring and auditability
* reduces manpower dependency
* enables future intelligent warehouse automation

# PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

## Centralized Orchestration

C2 is the single orchestration authority for automation workflows.

## Realtime Visibility

All workflow states, robot status, and operational events must be visible in realtime.

## Reliability First

Operational stability is prioritized over feature richness.

## Traceability by Design

All actions, commands, and workflow transitions must be auditable.

## Human-Assisted Automation

Humans handle exceptions while automation handles repetitive operational workflows.

## Integration-Driven

Architecture must support:

* multi-vendor systems
* evolving APIs
* future device onboarding

## Scalability

Architecture must support:

* additional robots
* additional SMARTBoxes
* future multi-site expansion

## Security & Safety

Operational systems must be:

* authenticated
* auditable
* resilient
* safety-aware
